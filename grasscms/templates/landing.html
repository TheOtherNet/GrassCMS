What is GrassCMS?

GrassCMS is the ultimate page edition system, but it's much more!
With grasscms you can do everything from a family photo album (checkout this) to a corporate website.
It's also the perfect tool for web designers, to make sketches in real time, your client will be impressed!

How does it work?

By using the lastest javascript and html5 technologies, and a powerful convertion system, grasscms allows you to create and import any number of elements on your page and modify them to your fits (resize, move, opacity...) on a complete and intuitive wisiwig interface.
